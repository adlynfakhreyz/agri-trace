Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgriPay?

AgriPay is our secure digital wallet service designed specifically for agricultural transactions. It allows farmers, buyers, and sellers to manage funds and make payments within the AgriTrace ecosystem.

How do I create an AgriPay account?

It's easy! Just click the "Link My AgriPay Wallet" button above. We'll automatically use your AgriTrace username to create your AgriPay account.

Is AgriPay secure?

Yes! AgriPay employs bank-level security protocols, two-factor authentication, and encrypted transactions to ensure your funds are always protected.

Will there be transaction fees?

AgriPay transactions within the AgriTrace platform are fee-free. Only external withdrawals to bank accounts incur standard processing fees.
